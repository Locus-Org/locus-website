No. 01 — First Edition

The Kinetic
Sand Table.

A studio piece in walnut, brushed brass, and silica. Made to order. First edition of 120.

The piece

Underneath glass, a steel bearing rests in fine silica. It moves slowly — sometimes for hours — tracing compositions the studio has named, or new ones it chooses on its own.

The frame is solid walnut, joined by hand. The bearing is brushed brass, gravity-driven and quiet. There is no app to open and nothing to charge. It begins when you switch it on at the back, and ends when you switch it off.

Compositions

Four named patterns,
and one that listens.

The Table holds four compositions chosen by the studio, and a fifth — Drift — which observes the room and writes its own.

01 Spiral
02 Field

N° 05 — Drift

An open composition, listening.

In Drift, the Table takes its cues from the room: the hour, the sound, the months it has lived with you. It proposes patterns it has never proposed before, and keeps the ones you watch for a while. This is the intelligence we put inside the object. We hope it will feel like company.

In the room

It does not
ask for attention.

The Table is built to sit beside the life of the room, not above it. It is the size of a low coffee table. It draws while you read, while you cook, while you host. You may forget about it for hours and find a new pattern waiting when you look back.

A few details

Considered
where it counts.

The brass bearing arrives in a small wooden box, alongside a soft brush, a microfibre, and a card with your edition number. A quiet keepsake to start a long life together.
