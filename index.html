Scroll No. 01 — The Kinetic Table
A note from the studio

Locus makes a small family of quiet machines for rooms that hold their breath.

Each object carries an intelligence — not so it can perform, but so it can settle into the hour, the season, the way the light falls. We think of it as craft, slowed down until the seam disappears.

The first object is a table. Underneath its glass, a single bearing draws the sand into patterns that the room itself helps choose. It is never finished, and never the same twice.

No. 01

The Kinetic
Sand Table.

A walnut frame, hand-blown glass, fine silica, and a single brass bearing that thinks before it moves.

The Table draws in slow time. Four named compositions, and a fifth that listens to the room and writes its own. Made to order. First edition of 120.
